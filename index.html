Who are We?

Welcome! I’m Malak Alfurais, a dedicated physiotherapist committed to helping patients regain mobility, manage pain, and enhance their overall well-being. With experience at A Plus Hospital and Avcılar Private Hospital, I have worked in diverse healthcare settings, applying advanced therapeutic techniques to support effective recovery. My approach focuses on personalized care, ensuring that each patient receives a treatment plan tailored to their unique needs. I have also pursued specialized training in Cupping Therapy and Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization (IASTM) to provide the best possible care. Whether you’re recovering from an injury or looking to improve your movement, I’m here to support you on your journey to better health. Let’s work together to achieve your goals!

FAQs

1. How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your condition. Some patients see improvement in a few sessions, while others may need long-term therapy.

2. Do I need a doctor’s referral?

No, you don’t need a referral. You can book an appointment directly.

3. Does insurance cover physiotherapy?

Coverage depends on your insurance provider. Please check with them for details.

4. What should I wear to my session?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows free movement, such as gym wear.
